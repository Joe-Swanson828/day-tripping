# Aesthetic Direction Reference

When generating a design system, use these as inspiration for SPECIFIC, BOLD choices. Never mix-and-match generically — commit to a direction and push it further than feels safe.

## Direction Examples

### Psychedelic / 1960s-70s Counterculture
- **Fonts**: Outward (display), Righteous, Fredoka, Shrikhand; paired with something readable like Nunito or Quicksand
- **Colors**: Saturated warm spectrum — burnt orange, electric violet, hot pink, acid green, golden yellow on deep indigo or charcoal
- **Gradients**: Multi-stop gradients mimicking tie-dye or lava lamp movement
- **Radius**: Mix of very round (pills, circles) and organic/wavy shapes
- **Textures**: Noise overlays, grain, wavy SVG dividers, halftone dot patterns
- **Motion**: Slow, flowing transitions; elements that breathe/pulse; parallax scroll layers
- **Special**: Wavy text effects, radial gradient glows, mandala-like decorative elements

### Retro-Futuristic / Synthwave
- **Fonts**: Orbitron, Audiowide, Rajdhani; paired with IBM Plex Sans or Space Mono
- **Colors**: Neon cyan, hot pink, electric purple on dark navy/black; chrome silver accents
- **Gradients**: Linear bottom-to-top sunset (dark blue → purple → pink → orange)
- **Textures**: Grid lines receding to a vanishing point, scanline overlays, chromatic aberration
- **Motion**: Glitch effects, neon flicker, smooth swoops

### Brutalist / Raw
- **Fonts**: Archivo Black, Space Grotesk (Bold only), Courier Prime
- **Colors**: Black, white, one aggressive accent (red, yellow, or cyan). No gradients.
- **Borders**: Thick (3-4px), black, visible everywhere
- **Radius**: 0px. Everything is sharp.
- **Shadows**: Harsh drop shadows with no blur (4px 4px 0 #000)
- **Motion**: Minimal. Instant transitions. No easing.

### Organic / Natural
- **Fonts**: Bitter, Lora, or DM Serif Display; paired with Source Sans 3 or Libre Franklin
- **Colors**: Earth tones — forest green, terracotta, sandstone, cream, charcoal
- **Radius**: Soft but not perfectly round — slightly irregular-feeling
- **Textures**: Paper grain, watercolor washes, botanical line art
- **Motion**: Gentle, organic easing (spring physics); elements that feel alive

### Luxury / Editorial
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or Bodoni Moda; paired with Montserrat or Raleway
- **Colors**: Near-black, off-white/cream, gold or champagne accent. Restrained palette.
- **Spacing**: Very generous whitespace. Let things breathe.
- **Borders**: Hairline (0.5-1px) or none. Subtle.
- **Shadows**: None or extremely subtle. Depth through layered opacity.
- **Motion**: Slow, confident reveals. Smooth page transitions.

### Playful / Toy-Like
- **Fonts**: Baloo 2, Fredoka One, Rubik; paired with Nunito or Poppins
- **Colors**: Bright primaries + pastels — coral, sky blue, mint, sunny yellow, lavender
- **Radius**: Very round (16-24px), pill shapes everywhere
- **Shadows**: Colorful drop shadows (not gray — use tinted shadows matching the element)
- **Motion**: Bouncy easing, wobble effects, scale-up on hover
- **Special**: Illustrated icons, emoji accents, confetti moments

### Dark Command / Hacker
- **Fonts**: JetBrains Mono, Fira Code, or IBM Plex Mono; body in Inter or system mono
- **Colors**: True black (#0A0A0A) base, phosphor green (#00FF41) or amber (#FFB000) accent, dim gray text
- **Radius**: 2-4px, technical and precise
- **Borders**: 1px subtle borders, often using rgba for translucency
- **Motion**: Fast, precise. Terminal-style text reveals. Cursor blink effects.

## Font Pairing Strategies

**High contrast = interesting.** Combine:
- Serif display + geometric sans body
- Rounded display + monospace body
- Heavy slab + thin sans body
- Script/handwritten accent + clean geometric everything else

**Load only what you need**: Specify exact weights (e.g., Playfair Display 400, 700 — not the full family).

## Color Contrast Quick Reference

On dark backgrounds (#1a1a2e or similar):
- White text (#FFFFFF): ~15:1 ✅
- Light gray (#E0E0E0): ~12:1 ✅
- Medium gray (#9CA3AF): ~5.5:1 ✅ (large text)
- Neon green (#00FF41): ~8:1 ✅

On light backgrounds (#FAFAFA or similar):
- Near-black (#1A1A2E): ~15:1 ✅
- Dark gray (#374151): ~10:1 ✅
- Medium gray (#6B7280): ~5:1 ✅ (large text)
- Saturated colors vary — always verify.
